About Us

Since their opening in 1925, weddings have been a beautiful tradition at the Hawthorne Hotel. With venues that offer timeless elegance and the finest attention to detail, your wedding will reflect your individual style in this classic setting. Take your vows in the beautiful outdoors or in the luxurious space of several ceremony sites on the property. The Ballroom is ideal for walking down the aisle, with 19-foot ceilings, magnificent Palladian windows, and an expansive interior radiating beauty. Meet your love under an archway and a stunning gold sconce that mirrors the subtle décor shimmering throughout the room. For a grand reception, the Essex Room combines effortless style and a comfortable environment while the Pickman Room is made for an intimate party with views to die for of Salem Common. With renowned catering and wonderful spaces to make unforgettable memories, the Hawthorne Hotel will impress you and your guests on the big day.
